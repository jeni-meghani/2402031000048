Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with TradePro?
Getting started is easy! Simply click the "Sign Up" button, fill out the registration form, verify your email, and complete the KYC verification process. Once approved, you can fund your account and start trading immediately.
What are the minimum deposit requirements?
The minimum deposit varies by account type. For standard accounts, the minimum deposit is $100. Premium accounts require a minimum of $1,000 but offer additional features like lower spreads and personalized support.
How do I fund my trading account?
We support multiple funding methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and popular e-wallets like PayPal, Skrill, and Neteller. All transactions are processed within 24 hours for most methods.
What are the withdrawal processing times?
Withdrawals are typically processed within 24 hours after approval. First withdrawals may take longer due to additional verification requirements. Bank transfers may take 3-5 business days to appear in your account.
Is my money safe with TradePro?
Absolutely. We maintain segregation of client funds in top-tier banks, use 256-bit SSL encryption, and are regulated by multiple financial authorities. Our platform has been independently audited and has a clean regulatory record.
What trading instruments are available?
We offer over 10,000 trading instruments including stocks, forex, commodities, cryptocurrencies, indices, and ETFs. Our product range is continuously expanding to meet trader demands.
How do your fees compare to other platforms?
We offer highly competitive pricing with spreads starting from 0.1 pips on major currency pairs. Stock trading is commission-free, and we don't charge inactivity fees. Our transparent pricing model ensures no hidden costs.
Can I trade on mobile devices?
Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. It offers all the features of our desktop platform including advanced charts, real-time data, and push notifications.
What is the maximum leverage available?
We offer flexible leverage options up to 1:500 for professional traders. However, we recommend using lower leverage to manage risk appropriately. Leverage limits may vary based on your location and account type.
Do you offer demo accounts?
Yes, we provide unlimited demo accounts with $100,000 virtual funds. This allows you to practice trading strategies and familiarize yourself with the platform without risking real money.
